JengaBiz is an enterprise resource planning web based application that will enable business owners to record their day to day business transactions and compute their daily cash balance based on sales made and expenses incurred. Nairobi Housing

Nairobi Housing provides details on potential areas of residence based one's budget or salary. By providing either of these two details , list of prospective neighbourhoods that match the user's income or budget is provided. This list can be narrowed down further to a specific subcounty, ward and to specific housing types.
